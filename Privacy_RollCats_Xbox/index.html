Privacy Policy

Game: RollCats

Developer: Hoglet Interactive

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled in the game RollCats. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

RollCats does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

The game does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (such as name, email address, or account information)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports linked to users
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Third-Party Services

RollCats does not use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or data tracking.

Platform services provided by Microsoft (such as Xbox system features) operate under Microsoft’s own privacy policy.

Children’s Privacy

RollCats does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or users of any age. Because no personal data is collected, no special data handling is required.

Changes to This Policy

If the game’s data practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
